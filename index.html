About SSN

Sri Sivasubramaniya Nadar College of Engineering (SSNCE) is the outcome of the vision and initiative of Dr. Shiv Nadar, Chairman, HCL Technologies and a pioneer in the field of Information Technology. The Institution stands out as a premier center of higher learning with a mission of pursuing excellence in education and research. SSN institutions, with its diverse and dynamic community of students offer a distinctive combination of some of the finest undergraduate, postgraduate and research programmes. The campus has been interconnected through wired as well as wireless (Wi-Fi) Network. In addition, online access is provided to reference books and journals. This has enabled SSN Institutions to adopt innovative teaching & learning methodologies. The institution is certified with ISO and accredited by NBA and NAAC. SSN has been consistently proving its class by retaining the top position among Anna University affiliated Engineering Colleges in Tamilnadu.

Department of Mathematics

The department of Mathematics has 17 faculty members with doctorates working on diverse areas such as Fluid Mechanics, Graph Theory, Coding Theory, Image Processing, Fuzzy Theory, Queuing Theory, Functional Analysis, Stochastic Process, Algebra and Topology. The department is a recognized research center of Anna University, Chennai and students are pursuing Ph.D. programme.

Do people say that you're great at understanding others with no words being spoken? Or are you simply someone who's looking for some fun? Well look no more! Come, participate in the Dumb-C event at Exlog 2020, the State level Mathematical Symposium organised by the Department of Mathematics at SSN College of Engineering. This isn't just your everyday charades game. It's got different rounds of exciting variations of Dumb-C, each with a Mathematical twist. You don't want to miss this one. Be there on 22nd February to enjoy the fun.
Rules:-
Maximum of 3 per team and minimum of 2.
Judges decisions are final
3 Rounds:-
1st : An aptitude test to test your talent.
2nd : A mathematical term enacted by a teammate and guessed by the others on the team. Team that takes the least time wins.
